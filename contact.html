FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers to questions we hear most often from prospective clients.

Do you work exclusively with government clients?
No. While government and public sector projects are a core strength, we also serve political leaders, parties, enterprises, and organizations across multiple sectors. Our three service pillars — Government Projects, Political Marketing, and IT Services — serve a broad range of clients.
How do you handle data confidentiality for political campaigns?
We operate under strict NDAs and internal data protection protocols for every political engagement. Campaign strategies, voter data, and communications are handled with absolute confidentiality. Our team members involved in political projects operate under segregated access controls.
What is the typical project timeline?
Timelines vary by project scope. A basic IT service or campaign setup can begin within 1–2 weeks. Government portal projects typically range from 3–12 months depending on complexity and integration requirements. We provide a detailed project schedule during the proposal stage.
Do you provide post-launch support and maintenance?
Absolutely. We offer comprehensive Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) and SLA-based support packages. Our 24/7 support team ensures mission-critical government systems remain operational and secure at all times. Maintenance packages are customizable to your needs.
Which states and regions do you serve?
We are headquartered in Hyderabad, Telangana, and serve clients across 15+ states in India. Our teams can operate on-site for critical government engagements, and we have delivery capabilities across the country. We also serve central government bodies and national-level projects.
